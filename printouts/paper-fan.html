How to Make Your Fan

  1. Print this page on regular paper (cardstock works even better)
  2. Cut along the outer dashed border
  3. Fold back and forth along each dashed panel line (accordion style)
  4. Pinch the bottom edge together
  5. Secure with tape, a staple, or rubber band
  6. Fan out the top and stay cool!
