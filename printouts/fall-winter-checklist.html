Fall & Winter HVAC Checklist

Prepare your heating system for cold weather. Complete these tasks to stay warm, safe, and energy-efficient.

Fall Preparation Tasks

Schedule professional fall furnace tune-up
Replace or clean air filter
Test heating system before cold weather arrives
Check and replace thermostat batteries
Inspect furnace flame (should be steady blue)
Clean around furnace/boiler (3ft clearance)
Seal air leaks around windows and doors
Inspect and clean chimney/flue (if applicable)
Reverse ceiling fan direction (clockwise)
Cover outdoor AC unit for winter
Bleed radiators (if you have hot water heat)

Winter Maintenance Tasks

Check air filter monthly (replace as needed)
Test carbon monoxide detectors monthly
Keep all vents and registers unblocked
Monitor for cold spots or uneven heating
Check weather stripping on exterior doors
Inspect attic insulation
Keep furnace area clear of storage/clutter
Monitor energy bills for unusual spikes
Listen for unusual furnace noises
Check pilot light or ignitor operation

When to Call a Pro

Furnace not producing heat
Yellow or flickering furnace flame
Strange smells (gas, burning, musty)
Carbon monoxide detector alarm
Frequent cycling on/off
Unusually high energy bills
