Winter Paper Snowflake Template

Cut out the circle, fold in half 3 times, then cut along the orange dashed shapes. Unfold to reveal your unique snowflake! Each one comes out different. Decorate your windows while your furnace keeps you warm.

How to Make Your Snowflake

  1. Cut out the circle along the solid border
  2. Fold in half along the horizontal dashed line
  3. Fold in half again along the vertical line
  4. Fold diagonally one more time
  5. Cut along the orange dashed shapes (or make your own!)
  6. Unfold carefully and enjoy!
