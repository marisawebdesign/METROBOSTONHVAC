Filter Replacement Schedule

1" Fiberglass:
Replace every 30 days
1" Pleated:
Replace every 60-90 days
2" Pleated:
Replace every 3-4 months
4" Pleated:
Replace every 6-12 months
HEPA Filters:
Replace per manufacturer specs

Check more often if you have pets, allergies, or during heavy use.
